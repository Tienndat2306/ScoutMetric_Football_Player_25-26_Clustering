Cap nhat GMM: confidence, covariance_type va ambiguous players

1. Bo sung Cluster Confidence cho GMM
- Trong run_gmm_clustering, sau khi fit GMM se goi predict_proba(scaled_data).
- Moi cau thu duoc gan them Cluster_Confidence = xac suat cao nhat trong cac cum.
- Cluster_Confidence duoc tra ve frontend trong response /api/cluster.
- Tooltip scatter chart hien thi them Confidence khi du lieu co truong nay.
- Cluster_Confidence khong duoc dung lam truc X/Y cua scatter chart.

2. Thu nhieu covariance_type cho GMM
- Bo sung fit_best_gmm_model trong clustering/football_player_clustering.py.
- GMM hien tai thu cac covariance_type:
  - full
  - tied
  - diag
  - spherical
- Voi moi K va moi covariance_type, he thong tinh BIC va AIC.
- Model GMM duoc chon theo BIC nho nhat.
- get_gmm_evaluation_data cung da tinh BIC theo covariance_type tot nhat cua tung K.

3. Hien thi cau thu nam giua nhieu cum
- Neu Cluster_Confidence < 0.65, cau thu duoc xem la ambiguous player.
- Backend tra ve toi da 10 cau thu co confidence thap nhat trong evaluation.ambiguous_players.
- Frontend hien thi bang "Cau thu nam giua nhieu cum" trong khu vuc danh gia chat luong.

4. Thong tin model GMM tren frontend
- evaluation.gmm_info tra ve:
  - selected_k.
  - selected_covariance_type.
  - bic.
  - aic.
  - init_params.
  - initialization_method.
  - candidate_count.
- Frontend hien thi card "GMM model" trong khu vuc danh gia.

5. Phuong phap khoi tao ban dau cua GMM
- GaussianMixture cua scikit-learn trong project dang dung mac dinh init_params = "kmeans".
- Nghia la cac tham so ban dau cua GMM duoc khoi tao dua tren K-Means truoc khi chay thuat toan EM.
- Sau khi khoi tao bang K-Means, GMM toi uu cac tham so bang Expectation-Maximization:
  - E-step: tinh xac suat moi diem thuoc tung Gaussian component.
  - M-step: cap nhat trong so, mean va covariance cua tung component.
- random_state = 42 duoc giu de ket qua on dinh hon giua cac lan chay.

6. Kiem tra
- Da chay python -m py_compile cho clustering/football_player_clustering.py va app/routes.py.
- Da chay node --check app/static/js/main.js.
- Da chay thu GMM voi Midfielder / Top 5 League / Style.
- Ket qua test: selected_k = 2, selected_covariance_type = diag, co Cluster_Confidence, co gmm_info va co ambiguous_players.

- The player cards trong compare khong con hien thi diem tong hop trung binh mo ho.
- Uu tien hien thi chi so tong hop co cong thuc ro rang, vi du Expected Output = Non-penalty xG + Expected assists (xA).
- Neu backend da tra ve cot tong hop nhu Expected Output, Ball Winning Actions, Finishing, Build-up hoac Defending thi dung truc tiep gia tri do.
- Neu chua co cot tong hop nhung co du cot thanh phan, frontend tu tinh lai theo cong thuc.

- API tra them selected_features va chart.js uu tien selected_features khi chon truc scatter/summary de khong bi anh huong boi thu tu key JSON.

4. Kiem tra

Sua loi scatter chart voi criteria Duel

1. Nguyen nhan
- API Duel cho Midfielder va Defender van tra du 2 cot numeric: Aerial duels won va Duels won.
- Loi "At least two numeric features are required to draw the scatter chart" den tu frontend chart.js.
- Ham numericFeatureKeys truoc do chi kiem tra typeof rows[0][key] === 'number', nen neu dong dau tien trong response co gia tri null/string numeric thi chart ket luan sai rang khong du cot so.

2. Thay doi
- chart.js nay xac dinh cot numeric bang cach kiem tra toan bo rows voi Number.isFinite(Number(value)).
- Khi ve scatter, cac diem co gia tri truc X/Y khong hop le se bi bo qua thay vi lam hong ca chart.
- Neu khong con diem hop le nao, frontend hien thong bao ro hon: No valid numeric player values are available for the selected chart axes.

3. Kiem tra
- Da test /api/load_raw_data voi Midfielder va Defender, criteria Duel, tren cac league hien co.
- Da chay node --check app/static/js/chart.js.
- Da render thu / va /compare bang Flask test client, deu tra ve 200.
